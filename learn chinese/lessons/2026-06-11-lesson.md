## 3. Character Writing — 10 min

Write each character 5× by hand. (Your trainer app's stroke-order tab has 負, 經, 找.)

**負 (fù) — responsible / to bear**
Radical: 貝 (shell = money) under ⺈ (a person bending over). Memory hook: *a person bent over carrying money — bearing responsibility.*

**經 (jīng) — to pass through / classic** (in 經驗)
Components: 糸 (silk) + 巠 (underground stream). Hook: *threads of silk running through everything — experience runs through you.*

**找 (zhǎo) — to look for**
Components: 扌 (hand) + 戈 (spear). Hook: *hand holding a spear, hunting for something — job hunting.*

**面 (miàn) — face** (in 面試)
A pictograph of a face in a frame. Hook: *an interview is face-to-face.*

**會 (huì) — can / meeting / will** (in 機會)
Hook: 人 (people) gathered under a roof over 曰 (speaking) — *a meeting; where people meet, opportunity (機會) happens.*

## 4. Grammar Pattern — 10 min

### 在 + place + verb — "do something AT a place"

The 在-phrase comes **before** the verb, never after.

1. 我在 Acer 工作。— Wǒ zài Acer gōngzuò. — I work at Acer.
2. 我在台北住了十年。— Wǒ zài Táiběi zhùle shí nián. — I've lived in Taipei ten years.
3. 我在健身房運動。— Wǒ zài jiànshēnfáng yùndòng. — I work out at the gym.
4. 我在咖啡廳寫東西。— Wǒ zài kāfēitīng xiě dōngxi. — I write stuff at a café.
5. 你在哪裡工作?— Nǐ zài nǎlǐ gōngzuò? — Where do you work? (TW: 哪裡, not 哪儿)

## 5. Dialogue — 10 min

**Scene: meeting someone new at a Taipei café.** Read both roles aloud; A = stranger, B = you.

> **A:** 你好,我是小林。你叫什麼名字?
> Nǐ hǎo, wǒ shì Xiǎo Lín. Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
> Hi, I'm Xiao Lin. What's your name?
>
> **B:** 你好,我叫 Clark。很高興認識你。
> Nǐ hǎo, wǒ jiào Clark. Hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ.
> Hi, I'm Clark. Nice to meet you.
>
> **A:** 你在台灣做什麼工作?
> Nǐ zài Táiwān zuò shénme gōngzuò?
> What do you do in Taiwan?
>
> **B:** 我做行銷,負責遊戲筆電的文案。
> Wǒ zuò xíngxiāo, fùzé yóuxì bǐdiàn de wén'àn.
> I do marketing — I'm responsible for gaming laptop copy.
>
> **A:** 哇,好酷!做多久了?
> Wā, hǎo kù! Zuò duōjiǔ le?
> Wow, cool! How long have you done that?
>
> **B:** 十年了。現在我在找新的機會。
> Shí nián le. Xiànzài wǒ zài zhǎo xīn de jīhuì.
> Ten years. Right now I'm looking for new opportunities.

**Pronunciation notes:** 行銷 xíngxiāo — both rise then stay high; don't let 銷 fall. 認識 rènshi — second syllable is neutral/light. 好酷 hǎo kù — very common TW filler for "so cool."

**Follow-up questions to answer yourself:** 你喜歡你的工作嗎?你想找什麼樣的工作?
