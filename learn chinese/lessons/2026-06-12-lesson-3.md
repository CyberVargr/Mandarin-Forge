- **放鬆一下** — adding 一下 softens the verb. Super natural in speech.

## 3. Character Writing — 10 min

Write each character 5× by hand. Study the components and memory hook first.

---

**心 (xīn) — heart / mind**

The simplest, most powerful character. Pictograph of a heart with three drops of blood.

Uses: 心情 (mood), 小心 (be careful), 用心 (put your heart into it), 心裡 (inside your heart), 開心 (happy — "open heart")

Memory hook: *A heart with three droplets — blood and feeling.*

---

**靜 (jìng) — quiet, still**

Components: 青 (qīng = blue-green, youth, serene) + 爭 (zhēng = compete, struggle)

Memory hook: *Blue-green nature finally wins the struggle — everything goes still.*

Full word: 安靜 (ānjìng) = peaceful quiet (安 = peace + 靜 = still)

---

**感 (gǎn) — feel, sense**

Components: 咸 (xián = all, salty) + 心 (heart at the bottom)

Memory hook: *Your heart tasting everything — feeling with your whole self.*

Uses: 靈感 (inspiration), 感覺 (feeling, sensation), 感謝 (gratitude), 感動 (moved/touched)

---

**美 (měi) — beautiful, beauty**

Components: 羊 (yáng = sheep) on top of 大 (dà = big/person)

Memory hook: *A person wearing a big sheep headdress at a ceremony — beautiful in the ancient sense.*

Uses: 美食 (delicious food), 好美 (so beautiful), 美工 (graphic design/art), 美術 (fine arts)

## 4. Grammar Pattern — 10 min

### 覺得 (juéde) — "I think / I feel"

This is one of the most useful patterns for self-expression. Use it for opinions, feelings, and reactions.

**Structure:** 我覺得 + [description]

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 我覺得這裡很安靜。| Wǒ juéde zhèlǐ hěn ānjìng. | I feel this place is very quiet. |
| 我覺得找工作很難。| Wǒ juéde zhǎo gōngzuò hěn nán. | I think job hunting is hard. |
| 我覺得今天的咖啡很好喝。| Wǒ juéde jīntiān de kāfēi hěn hǎohē. | I think today's coffee tastes great. |
| 你覺得怎麼樣？| Nǐ juéde zěnmeyàng? | What do you think? / How do you feel? |
| 我覺得這個創意很有趣。| Wǒ juéde zhège chuàngyì hěn yǒuqù. | I think this idea is interesting. |

**Upgrade — combine with 之後 from Lesson 2:**

- 健身之後，我覺得很放鬆。
  *Jiànshēn zhīhòu, wǒ juéde hěn fàngsōng.*
  "After training, I feel relaxed."

- 喝完咖啡之後，我覺得有靈感。
  *Hē wán kāfēi zhīhòu, wǒ juéde yǒu línggǎn.*
  "After finishing my coffee, I feel inspired."

## 5. Dialogue — 10 min

**At a Taipei indie café — ordering and small talk.** Role A = 你 (Clark), Role B = 店員 (staff)

---

> **B:** 你好，請問要喝什麼？
> *Nǐ hǎo, qǐngwèn yào hē shénme?*
> Hi, what would you like to drink?

> **A:** 請問有手沖嗎？
> *Qǐngwèn yǒu shǒuchōng ma?*
> Do you have pour-over coffee?

> **B:** 有喔，今天的豆子是衣索比亞，花香帶點果酸。
> *Yǒu ō, jīntiān de dòuzi shì Yīsuǒbǐyà, huā xiāng dài diǎn guǒ suān.*
> Yes! Today's bean is Ethiopian, floral with a bit of fruit acidity.

> **A:** 聽起來不錯，我要一杯。這裡可以坐多久？
> *Tīng qǐlái búcuò, wǒ yào yī bēi. Zhèlǐ kěyǐ zuò duō jiǔ?*
> Sounds good, I'll have one. How long can I sit here?

> **B:** 沒有時間限制，請隨便坐。
> *Méiyǒu shíjiān xiànzhì, qǐng suíbiàn zuò.*
> No time limit, sit as long as you like.

> **A:** 太好了，謝謝！這裡真的很安靜，我喜歡。
> *Tài hǎo le, xièxiè! Zhèlǐ zhēnde hěn ānjìng, wǒ xǐhuān.*
> That's great, thank you! It's really quiet here, I like it.

---

**Pronunciation notes:**
- **有喔** (yǒu ō) — the 喔 softens the confirmation. Very natural Taiwan speech particle, like a warm "yep!"
- **帶點** (dài diǎn) = "carries a little" — you'll hear this describing food and drink all the time.
- **隨便坐** (suíbiàn zuò) = "sit freely / however you like" — extremely common Taiwan hospitality phrase.
- **真的很** (zhēnde hěn) = "really very" — use this to emphasize naturally.

**Follow-up questions (answer aloud):**

1. 你最喜歡什麼咖啡？黑咖啡、拿鐵、還是手沖？
2. 你在哪裡最有靈感？咖啡廳、家、還是健身房之後？

## 6. Self-Expression + Mini Quiz — 10 min

### Today's self-expression sentences

These are things you can actually say about your real life right now.

---

**1. 我喜歡在安靜的咖啡廳寫文案。**
*Wǒ xǐhuān zài ānjìng de kāfēitīng xiě wén'àn.*
"I like writing copy in a quiet café."

- Casual: 我在咖啡廳寫東西，比較有感覺。("I write stuff in cafés — feels better.")
- Polished: 安靜的環境有助於提升我的創作靈感。("A quiet environment helps boost my creative inspiration.")

---

**2. 我現在在換工作，心情有點複雜。**
*Wǒ xiànzài zài huàn gōngzuò, xīnqíng yǒudiǎn fùzá.*
"I'm currently changing jobs, so my mood is a bit complicated."

- Note: 複雜 (fùzá) = complicated, mixed. A great word for complex emotional states.
- Casual: 換工作這件事，說複雜也複雜，說簡單也簡單。

---

**3. 我需要放鬆，也需要靈感。**
*Wǒ xūyào fàngsōng, yě xūyào línggǎn.*
"I need to relax, and I also need inspiration."

- Pattern: A，也 B = "A, and also B" — very natural in Chinese.
