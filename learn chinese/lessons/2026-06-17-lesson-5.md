| 9 | 外帶 | wàidài | takeaway (Taiwan term) | 外帶還是內用？Wàidài háishi nèiyòng? |
| 10 | 加 | jiā | to add | 可以加辣嗎？Kěyǐ jiā là ma? |

**Taiwan-specific notes:**
- **外帶** (wàidài) = takeaway — this is the Taiwan term. Mainland uses 外帶 or 外賣 (wàimài).
- **內用** (nèiyòng) = eat in — you'll hear 外帶還是內用？ at almost every food stall.
- **小吃** — pronounced xiǎochī, not "xiǎo chī." The compound is said quickly together.
- **份** — measure word for a portion/serving: 一份炒飯、一份滷肉飯

**Pronunciation notes:**
- 喜歡 — xǐ (3rd tone, dip) huān (1st, flat). The 歡 often sounds soft/unstressed in natural speech → xiǐhuān
- 風格 — fēng (1st, flat) gé (2nd, rising). Clean and crisp.
- 夜市 — yè (4th, falling) shì (4th, falling). Two falling tones. Short and punchy.
- 辣 — là (4th, sharp falling). Single syllable — say it with bite.

## ⟲ BLOCK 3 — Character Writing (10 min)

### Five characters today: 音、樂、市、外、帶

---

**1. 音 yīn** — sound, tone  
Used in: 音樂 (music), 聲音 (sound/voice), 音效 (sound effects)  
Radical: 音 (sound)  
Components: 立 (stand) + 日 (sun) — *"sound stands above the day"*  
Memory hook: 🔊 Sound rises like the sun — it lifts off the ground (立) and shines (日).  
Strokes: 9 | Tone: 1st (flat)  

---

**2. 樂 yuè / lè** — music (yuè) / happy (lè)  
Used in: 音樂 (music), 快樂 (happy), 娛樂 (entertainment)  
Radical: 木 (wood)  
Memory hook: 🎵 An instrument (木 = wood base) with strings above — a wooden instrument = music = joy.  
Strokes: 15 | Context tip: When it means *music*, say **yuè**. When it means *happy*, say **lè**.

---

**3. 市 shì** — market, city  
Used in: 夜市 (night market), 超市 (supermarket), 城市 (city)  
Radical: 巾 (cloth)  
Components: 亠 (lid/cover) + 巾 (cloth banner)  
Memory hook: 🏮 A banner (巾) hangs under a canopy (亠) — market stall!  
Strokes: 5 | Tone: 4th (falling)

---

**4. 外 wài** — outside, foreign, take-out  
Used in: 外帶 (takeaway), 外面 (outside), 外國 (foreign country)  
Radical: 夕 (evening)  
Components: 夕 (evening) + 卜 (oracle/divine)  
Memory hook: 🌙 Ancient fortune-tellers worked *outside* at evening (夕).  
Strokes: 5 | Tone: 4th (falling)

---

**5. 帶 dài** — to bring, belt, zone  
Used in: 外帶 (takeaway), 帶來 (bring here), 攜帶 (to carry)  
Radical: 巾 (cloth)  
Memory hook: 🎽 A cloth belt (巾 at bottom) that you *carry* around your waist.  
Strokes: 11 | Tone: 4th (falling)

**✍️ Practice:** Write each character 5 times by hand. Try to recall the memory hook on the 5th stroke.  
Then write: 音樂、夜市、外帶 — two times each as compound words.

## ⟲ BLOCK 4 — Grammar Pattern (10 min)

### Pattern: 除了…以外，也… (chúle… yǐwài, yě…)  
**Meaning: "Besides X, I also Y" / "Apart from X, also Y"**

**Structure:**
> 除了 [A] 以外，[subject] 也 [B]。

**Pronunciation note:** 除了 = chú (2nd, rising) le (neutral). The 以外 = yǐwài comes after the thing you're listing. Natural Taiwan speech often drops 以外 → just 除了 A，也 B.

---

**5 examples (read aloud — then cover and try to produce):**

1. 除了健身以外，我也喜歡聽音樂。  
   *Chúle jiànshēn yǐwài, wǒ yě xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè.*  
   "Besides going to the gym, I also like listening to music."

2. 除了拿鐵以外，我也喜歡手沖咖啡。  
   *Chúle nǎtiě yǐwài, wǒ yě xǐhuān shǒuchōng kāfēi.*  
   "Besides lattes, I also like pour-over coffee."

3. 除了台北以外，我也去過台南。  
   *Chúle Táiběi yǐwài, wǒ yě qùguò Táinán.*  
   "Besides Taipei, I've also been to Tainan."

4. 除了行銷以外，我也做文案。  
   *Chúle xíngxiāo yǐwài, wǒ yě zuò wénàn.*  
   "Besides marketing, I also do copywriting."

5. 除了電子音樂以外，我也喜歡鋼琴。  
   *Chúle diànzǐ yīnyuè yǐwài, wǒ yě xǐhuān gāngqín.*  
   "Besides electronic music, I also like piano."

## ⟲ BLOCK 5 — Speaking / Dialogue (10 min)

### Scene A — Talking about music with someone new

> 朋友: 你平常聽什麼音樂？  
> Péngyǒu: Nǐ píngcháng tīng shénme yīnyuè?  
> Friend: What kind of music do you usually listen to?

> 你: 我喜歡電子音樂，那種很有氛圍的感覺。  
> Nǐ: Wǒ xǐhuān diànzǐ yīnyuè, nà zhǒng hěn yǒu fēnwéi de gǎnjué.  
> You: I like electronic music — that kind with a very atmospheric feeling.

> 朋友: 有推薦嗎？  
> Péngyǒu: Yǒu tuījiàn ma?  
> Friend: Any recommendations?

> 你: 很難說，但我喜歡那種暗的、有未來感的風格。  
> Nǐ: Hěn nán shuō, dàn wǒ xǐhuān nà zhǒng àn de, yǒu wèilái gǎn de fēnggé.  
> You: Hard to say, but I like that dark, futuristic-feeling style.

---

### Scene B — Ordering at a night market stall (夜市點餐)

> 攤販: 歡迎，要吃什麼？  
> Tānfàn: Huānyíng, yào chī shénme?  
> Vendor: Welcome, what would you like?

> 你: 請問有滷肉飯嗎？  
> Nǐ: Qǐngwèn yǒu lǔròufàn ma?  
> You: Excuse me, do you have braised pork rice?

> 攤販: 有！要加蛋嗎？辣的還是不辣？  
> Tānfàn: Yǒu! Yào jiā dàn ma? Là de háishi bù là?  
> Vendor: Yes! Want to add an egg? Spicy or not spicy?

> 你: 不辣，可以加蛋。外帶，謝謝。  
> Nǐ: Bù là, kěyǐ jiā dàn. Wàidài, xièxie.  
> You: Not spicy, you can add an egg. Takeaway, thanks.

> 攤販: 好，一份滷肉飯加蛋，五十元。  
> Tānfàn: Hǎo, yī fèn lǔròufàn jiā dàn, wǔshí yuán.  
> Vendor: Ok, one braised pork rice with egg, 50 NT.

---

**Key phrases to own:**

| Phrase | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 外帶還是內用？ | wàidài háishi nèiyòng | Takeaway or eat in? |
| 可以加辣嗎？ | kěyǐ jiā là ma | Can you make it spicy? |
| 不辣，謝謝。 | bù là, xièxie | Not spicy, thanks. |
| 有推薦嗎？ | yǒu tuījiàn ma | Any recommendations? |
| 請問有___嗎？ | qǐngwèn yǒu ___ ma | Excuse me, do you have ___? |

**Pronunciation notes for dialogue:**
- 氛圍 fēnwéi — 1st + 2nd tone. *Atmosphere, vibe.* Useful word — keep it.
- 未來感 wèilái gǎn — "futuristic feel." 未來 = future, 感 = sense/feel. Very usable.
- 攤販 tānfàn — 1st + 4th. Night market vendor/stall.
- 滷肉飯 lǔròufàn — 3rd + 4th + 4th. Braised pork rice. Classic Taiwan dish.

**Follow-up questions (answer in Chinese if you can):**

1. 你最喜歡什麼台灣小吃？ (What Taiwan street food do you like most?)
2. 你通常外帶還是內用？ (Do you usually get takeaway or eat in?)
3. 你喜歡吃辣嗎？ (Do you like spicy food?)
