## 3. Character Writing — 10 min

Write each 5× by hand. Reuse 負 and 經 from yesterday first (2× each) — they're on the review list.

**肌 (jī) — muscle**
Components: 月 (flesh radical — body parts) + 几 (small table). Hook: *flesh stacked on a bench — muscle built at the bench press.*

**休 (xiū) — to rest**
Components: 亻(person) + 木 (tree). Hook: *a person leaning against a tree — resting.* One of the most logical characters in Chinese.

**練 (liàn) — to practice / train** (in 訓練)
Components: 糸 (silk) + 柬 (select). Hook: *selecting silk threads over and over — repetition is training.* Same 糸 as 經 from yesterday.

**蛋 (dàn) — egg** (in 蛋白質)
Top: 疋 (foot/roll) over 虫 (insect/creature). Hook: *a creature curled up inside — an egg.* 蛋白 = "egg white" = protein.

## 4. Grammar Pattern — 10 min

### Verb + 完 / 之後 — "after doing X, ..."

**[Action]之後，[result]。** — "After [action], [result]."

1. 健身之後，我覺得很舒服。— Jiànshēn zhīhòu, wǒ juéde hěn shūfú. — After working out, I feel great.
2. 練腿之後，走路很難。— Liàn tuǐ zhīhòu, zǒulù hěn nán. — After leg day, walking is hard.
3. 下班之後，我去健身房。— Xiàbān zhīhòu, wǒ qù jiànshēnfáng. — After work, I go to the gym.
4. 吃完之後，我想睡覺。— Chī wán zhīhòu, wǒ xiǎng shuìjiào. — After eating, I want to sleep.
5. 七月之後，我要找新工作。— Qīyuè zhīhòu, wǒ yào zhǎo xīn gōngzuò. — After July, I'm looking for a new job. (← yesterday's topic recycled)

## 5. Dialogue — 10 min

**At the gym — asking a trainer a question.** Role A = 你 (Clark), Role B = 教練

> **A:** 不好意思，請問一下。
> *Bù hǎoyìsi, qǐngwèn yíxià.* — Excuse me, can I ask something?
>
> **B:** 可以啊，怎麼了？
> *Kěyǐ a, zěnmele?* — Sure, what's up?
>
> **A:** 我的肩膀有一點痠，今天可以練胸嗎？
> *Wǒ de jiānbǎng yǒu yìdiǎn suān, jīntiān kěyǐ liàn xiōng ma?* — My shoulders are a bit sore. Can I train chest today?
>
> **B:** 可以，但是重量不要太重。
> *Kěyǐ, dànshì zhòngliàng búyào tài zhòng.* — Yes, but don't go too heavy.
>
> **A:** 好，那我先熱身。謝謝教練！
> *Hǎo, nà wǒ xiān rèshēn. Xièxiè jiàoliàn!* — OK, I'll warm up first. Thanks, coach!

**Pronunciation notes:** 不好意思 is THE Taiwan way to open any question to a stranger — own this phrase. 重量…重 zhòngliàng / zhòng: same character 重, same reading here (heavy). 熱身 rèshēn = warm up.

**Follow-up questions (answer aloud):**

1. 你一個禮拜去幾次健身房？(禮拜 lǐbài = week, very Taiwan)
2. 你最喜歡練什麼？胸、背、還是腿？
