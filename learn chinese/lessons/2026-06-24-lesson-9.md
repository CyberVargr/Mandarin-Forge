---

**Taiwan vocabulary notes:**

- **個人品牌** — used exactly like "personal brand" in English. Increasingly common in Taiwan's professional culture. 建立個人品牌 (jiànlì gèrén pǐnpái) = "building a personal brand" — a phrase you'll see on LinkedIn Taiwan all the time.

- **定位** — "positioning." Comes straight from marketing. Can be used for both product positioning and personal positioning. 我的定位是… = "My positioning is…" — clean, direct, professional.

- **專長** (zhuānchǎng) vs. **擅長** (shàncháng) — notice the difference:  
  - 專長 (noun) = "specialty / area of expertise" → 我的專長是…  
  - 擅長 (verb) = "to be good at / to excel in" → 我擅長…  
  Use both — they complement each other in pitches.

- **接案** — literally "to take on a case/project." The Taiwan word for doing freelance work. 接案子 (jiē ànzi) is slightly more casual. 接案 is clean and professional.

- **自由工作者** — the standard Taiwan term for freelancer. More formal than the borrowed 自由職業者. You'll see it in contracts, job boards, and LinkedIn profiles in Taiwan.

- **成果** — "results / accomplishments." More concrete and professional than just 結果 (jiéguǒ, which is more neutral). 用成果說話 = "let results speak" — a powerful phrase for a pitch.

- **跨領域** — "cross-domain." Combines 跨 (to cross/span) + 領域 (field/domain). Extremely useful for describing your background when you span hardware, copywriting, and brand strategy. 跨領域人才 = "cross-domain talent."

## ⟲ BLOCK 3 — Character Writing (10 min)

Write each character 5× by hand. Understand the structure before you write.

---

**1. 品 pǐn — product / grade / character / quality**  
Used in: 品牌 (brand), 品質 (quality), 作品 (work/creation), 商品 (goods)  
Structure: Three 口 (mouth) stacked — two on the bottom, one on top  
Memory hook: 👄 Three mouths talking about quality — 品 is what people *say* about the product.  
Strokes: 9 | Tone: 3rd (dip)  
*Note: 品 is one of those characters that tells you its meaning through its shape — multiplied 口 = multiple voices evaluating.*

---

**2. 牌 pái — brand / sign / card / placard**  
Used in: 品牌 (brand), 名牌 (famous brand / name tag), 招牌 (signboard — very Taiwan)  
Radical: 片 (flat piece / card, left side)  
Components: 片 (card/flat piece) + 卑 (right side)  
Memory hook: 🪧 A flat card (片) with a name on it — every 牌 is a sign that identifies something.  
Strokes: 12 | Tone: 2nd (rising)  
*Bonus Taiwan: 招牌菜 (zhāopái cài) = "signature dish" — brand logic applied to food.*

---

**3. 策 cè — strategy / plan / policy**  
Used in: 策略 (strategy), 政策 (policy), 對策 (countermeasure / response plan)  
Radical: 竹 (bamboo, top — bamboo strips were used for writing plans)  
Components: 竹 (bamboo, top) + 朿 (thorns, bottom)  
Memory hook: 🎋 Bamboo strips with thorny writing — a 策 is a sharp plan written on ancient material.  
Strokes: 12 | Tone: 4th (falling)

---

**4. 成 chéng — to accomplish / to complete / to become**  
Used in: 成果 (results/accomplishments), 完成 (to complete), 成功 (success), 成為 (to become)  
Radical: 戈 (spear/halberd)  
Memory hook: ⚔️ A spear that hits its mark — 成 is achievement, the moment something is *done*.  
Strokes: 6 | Tone: 2nd (rising)  
*高頻字: 成 appears in dozens of compounds. Master this one and unlock a cluster of vocabulary.*

---

**✍️ Practice compounds to write after characters:**  
品牌 (pǐnpái) · 策略 (cèlüè) · 成果 (chéngguǒ) · 招牌 (zhāopái) · 完成 (wánchéng)

## ⟲ BLOCK 4 — Grammar Pattern (10 min)

### Pattern: 從…到… (cóng...dào...)
**Meaning: "From X to Y" — showing range, breadth, or career span**

This pattern does something very specific and very powerful for a pitch: it shows **range**. It says: *I didn't just do one thing — I moved across a space.* Perfect for LinkedIn headlines, bios, and interview openers.

---

**Structure:**

> 從 [starting point / domain A]，到 [endpoint / domain B]  
> *Cóng [...], dào [...]*  
> "From X to Y / spanning from X to Y"

It can stand alone or connect to a main clause:

> 從 A 到 B，我 [did / built / learned / handled] [Z].

---

**Examples:**

| Chinese | Pinyin | English |
|---|---|---|
| 從文案寫作到品牌策略，我有十年的實際經驗。 | Cóng wénàn xiězuò dào pǐnpái cèlüè, wǒ yǒu shí nián de shíjì jīngyàn. | From copywriting to brand strategy, I have ten years of hands-on experience. |
| 從筆電到AI PC，我負責過各種產品的行銷文案。 | Cóng bǐdiàn dào AI PC, wǒ fùzéguò gèzhǒng chǎnpǐn de xíngxiāo wénàn. | From laptops to AI PCs, I've handled marketing copy for all kinds of products. |
| 從產品說明到活動文案，每一種都做過。 | Cóng chǎnpǐn shuōmíng dào huódòng wénàn, měi yī zhǒng dōu zuòguò. | From product descriptions to campaign copy — I've done all of it. |
| 從台灣市場到全球推廣，這段經驗很有價值。 | Cóng Táiwān shìchǎng dào quánqiú tuīguǎng, zhè duàn jīngyàn hěn yǒu jiàzhí. | From the Taiwan market to global promotion — this experience is very valuable. |
| 從一個人寫稿，到帶領整個文案方向，我都有做過。 | Cóng yīgèrén xiě gǎo, dào dàilǐng zhěnggè wénàn fāngxiàng, wǒ dōu yǒu zuòguò. | From writing solo, to leading an entire copy direction — I've done both. |

---

**Structure note:**  
You can use 從…到… twice in the same sentence to show two dimensions of range:  
> 從 [time A] 到 [time B]，從 [domain A] 到 [domain B]，我一直在做 [X]。  
> *"From year X to year Y, across X and Y domains, I've been doing Z."*

### Scene — Someone messages you on LinkedIn asking about your freelance services

**Role A = Clark (freelance copywriter / brand strategist). Role B = 台灣新創公司的行銷主管 (marketing manager at a Taiwanese startup)**

---

> **B:** 您好！看到您的個人頁面，想了解一下您目前是否有接案？  
> *Nín hǎo! Kàndào nín de gèrén yèmiàn, xiǎng liǎojiě yīxià nín mùqián shìfǒu yǒu jiē àn?*

> *Nín hǎo! Shì de, wǒ mùqián zhèngzài jiē àn, zhǔyào shì xíngxiāo wénàn hé pǐnpái cèlüè fāngmiàn de gōngzuò.*  
> Hello! Yes, I'm currently taking on freelance projects — mainly in marketing copywriting and brand strategy.

> **B:** 太好了。我們是一家做AI產品的新創，需要有人幫我們寫產品頁面和活動文案。請問您有這方面的經驗嗎？  
> *Tài hǎole. Wǒmen shì yījiā zuò AI chǎnpǐn de xīnchuàng, xūyào yǒurén bāng wǒmen xiě chǎnpǐn yèmiàn hé huódòng wénàn. Qǐngwèn nín yǒu zhè fāngmiàn de jīngyàn ma?*  
> Great. We're an AI startup and need someone to write product pages and campaign copy. Do you have experience in this area?

> **A:** 有的。我有十年的科技產品文案經驗，從筆電到AI PC都做過，非常熟悉如何把技術說得清楚又吸引人。  
> *Yǒu de. Wǒ yǒu shí nián de kējì chǎnpǐn wénàn jīngyàn, cóng bǐdiàn dào AI PC dōu zuòguò, fēicháng shúxī rúhé bǎ jìshù shuō de qīngchǔ yòu xīyǐn rén.*  
> Yes. I have ten years of experience writing copy for tech products — from laptops to AI PCs. I'm very familiar with how to explain technology in a way that's both clear and compelling.

> **B:** 聽起來很符合我們的需求！請問您通常怎麼報價？  
> *Tīng qǐlái hěn fúhé wǒmen de xūqiú! Qǐngwèn nín tōngcháng zěnme bàojià?*  
> That sounds like a great fit for us! How do you usually quote your rates?

> **A:** 我通常依專案範圍報價，可以先了解一下您的需求，再提供估價。您方便這週找時間聊聊嗎？  
> *Wǒ tōngcháng yī zhuān'àn fànwéi bàojià, kěyǐ xiān liǎojiě yīxià nín de xūqiú, zài tígōng gūjià. Nín fāngbiàn zhè zhōu zhǎo shíjiān liáoliao ma?*  
> I usually quote based on project scope. Let me first understand your needs, then I can provide an estimate. Would you be free to chat sometime this week?

> **B:** 好的，我明天下午有空，可以約個視訊嗎？  
> *Hǎo de, wǒ míngtiān xiàwǔ yǒu kòng, kěyǐ yuē gè shìxùn ma?*  
> Sure, I'm free tomorrow afternoon — can we schedule a video call?

> **A:** 當然可以！我也是，請您傳一下您方便的時間，我來確認。  
> *Dāngrán kěyǐ! Wǒ yě shì, qǐng nín chuán yīxià nín fāngbiàn de shíjiān, wǒ lái quèrèn.*  
> Of course! I'm available too — please send me a time that works for you and I'll confirm.

---

**Vocabulary from this dialogue:**

| Word | Pinyin | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 個人頁面 | gèrén yèmiàn | personal profile / profile page |
| 目前 | mùqián | currently / at present |
| 是否 | shìfǒu | whether or not (formal/written) |
| 新創 | xīnchuàng | startup (Taiwan shortform) |
| 活動文案 | huódòng wénàn | campaign copy / event copy |
| 熟悉 | shúxī | familiar with / well-versed in |
| 吸引人 | xīyǐn rén | compelling / attractive / engaging |
| 符合 | fúhé | to fit / to match / to meet (requirements) |
| 報價 | bàojià | to quote a price / pricing |
| 依…報價 | yī...bàojià | to price based on / to quote according to |
| 專案範圍 | zhuān'àn fànwéi | project scope |
| 估價 | gūjià | estimate / quote (noun) |
| 視訊 | shìxùn | video call (Taiwan — more common than 視頻通話) |

---

**Pronunciation notes:**

- **是否** (shìfǒu) — 4th+3rd. Formal/written "whether or not." Don't use 是不是 in professional messages — 是否 reads cleaner.
- **熟悉** (shúxī) — 2nd+1st. Very useful: 非常熟悉 (very familiar with) is a strong phrase for pitches.
- **符合** (fúhé) — 2nd+2nd. "To fit / meet requirements." 符合需求 (fúhé xūqiú) = "fits the need" — learn this pair.
- **視訊** (shìxùn) — 4th+4th. Taiwan's word for video call. Mainland says 視頻通話. 約個視訊 = "schedule a video call."
- Watch: 目前 (mùqián) vs. 目前正在 (mùqián zhèngzài) — adding 正在 signals you're actively doing something right now.
