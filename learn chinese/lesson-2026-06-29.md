### Example Sentences

*(Wǒ xiàn zài zài jī jí qiú zhí.)*

## ✍️ BLOCK 3 — Character Writing (10 minutes)

### Today's Characters: 5 Job Hunt Kanji

---

**1. 職** (zhí) — position / job

- Meaning: professional post, role
- Radical: 耳 (ear) — the ear hears orders at work
- Components: 耳 + 音 (sound) + 戈 (weapon variant)
- Memory Hook: *"You hear (耳) the call to battle (戈) — that's your job (職)."*
- Words: 職位 (zhí wèi) = position, 求職 (qiú zhí) = job hunt
- Stroke count: 18 — write slowly, center the 耳 radical on the left

---

**2. 徵** (zhēng) — to recruit / to draft / sign

- Meaning: to call for, recruit, apply
- Radical: 彳 (step) — moving toward something
- Memory Hook: *"You take steps (彳) toward a new chapter — 應徵 means applying."*
- Words: 應徵 (yìng zhēng) = to apply, 徵才 (zhēng cái) = recruiting talent
- Stroke count: 15 — common in job listings on 104.com.tw

---

**3. 驗** (yàn) — to verify / experience

- Meaning: experience, to test or verify
- Radical: 馬 (horse) — horses are tested for speed and strength
- Memory Hook: *"A horse (馬) is tested (驗) before battle — your experience proves you."*
- Words: 經驗 (jīng yàn) = experience, 驗證 (yàn zhèng) = verification
- Stroke count: 23 — complex, practice the 馬 component first

---

**4. 強** (qiáng) — strong / strength

- Meaning: strong, powerful, to strengthen
- Radical: 弓 (bow) — a bow requires strength to draw
- Memory Hook: *"Pull the bow (弓) hard — that takes 強 (strength)."*
- Words: 強項 (qiáng xiàng) = strength/strong suit, 強調 (qiáng diào) = to emphasize
- Stroke count: 12 — clean and learnable

---

**5. 案** (àn) — case / project / file

- Meaning: a case, plan, or project/file
- Radical: 木 (wood/tree) — a wooden desk where cases are filed
- Memory Hook: *"Files sit on the wooden (木) desk — that's a 案 (case)."*
- Words: 文案 (wén àn) = copy/copywriting, 接案 (jiē àn) = take freelance work, 方案 (fāng àn) = plan/solution
- Stroke count: 10 — practice 木 base first

## 📐 BLOCK 4 — Grammar Pattern (10 minutes)

### Pattern: **對……有興趣** — "to be interested in..."

**Structure:**  
> 我對 [topic/thing] 有興趣。  
> *(Wǒ duì [topic] yǒu xìng qù.)*  
> I'm interested in [topic].

**More specific version:**  
> 我對 [topic] 比較有興趣。  
> "I'm more interested in / I tend to be interested in..."

---

### 5 Natural Examples

1. 我對行銷和品牌策略很有興趣。  
   *(Wǒ duì xíng xiāo hé pǐn pái cè lüè hěn yǒu xìng qù.)*  
   → I'm very interested in marketing and brand strategy.

2. 我對電競產業比較有興趣。  
   *(Wǒ duì diàn jìng chǎn yè bǐ jiào yǒu xìng qù.)*  
   → I'm more interested in the gaming industry.

3. 你對什麼樣的工作有興趣？  
   *(Nǐ duì shén me yàng de gōng zuò yǒu xìng qù?)*  
   → What kind of work are you interested in?

4. 我對外商或國際業務的公司有興趣。  
   *(Wǒ duì wài shāng huò guó jì yè wù de gōng sī yǒu xìng qù.)*  
   → I'm interested in foreign companies or companies with international operations.

5. 我對自由接案也有興趣。  
   *(Wǒ duì zì yóu jiē àn yě yǒu xìng qù.)*  
   → I'm also interested in freelancing.

## 🎭 BLOCK 5 — Speaking / Dialogue (10 minutes)

### Dialogue: Job Interview at a Taipei Startup

**Setting:** You're interviewing at a mid-sized Taiwan tech/marketing company. The interviewer (面試官, miàn shì guān) opens.

---

**面試官:** 可以先做一下自我介紹嗎？  
*(Kě yǐ xiān zuò yī xià zì wǒ jiè shào ma?)*  
→ Could you start with a self-introduction?

**Clark:** 好的。我叫Clark，在台灣住了十年。我之前在宏碁工作，負責Predator電競品牌的英文文案，包括產品頁、活動行銷、還有全球發表會的內容。  
*(Hǎo de. Wǒ jiào Clark, zài Táiwān zhù le shí nián. Wǒ zhī qián zài Hóng qí gōng zuò, fù zé Predator diàn jìng pǐn pái de yīng wén wén àn, bāo kuò chǎn pǐn yè, huó dòng xíng xiāo, hái yǒu quán qiú fā biǎo huì de nèi róng.)*  
→ Sure. My name is Clark. I've lived in Taiwan for ten years. Previously I worked at Acer, handling English copywriting for the Predator gaming brand — including product pages, campaign marketing, and global launch content.

---

**面試官:** 你覺得你最大的強項是什麼？  
*(Nǐ juéde nǐ zuì dà de qiáng xiàng shì shén me?)*  
→ What do you think your biggest strength is?

**Clark:** 我擅長把複雜的技術規格轉化成讓消費者有感覺的語言。我特別喜歡寫那種讓人一看就想要的產品文案。  
*(Wǒ shàn cháng bǎ fù zá de jì shù guī gé zhuǎn huà chéng ràng xiāo fèi zhě yǒu gǎn jué de yǔ yán. Wǒ tè bié xǐ huān xiě nà zhǒng ràng rén yī kàn jiù xiǎng yào de chǎn pǐn wén àn.)*  
→ I'm good at converting complex technical specs into language that resonates with consumers. I especially love writing product copy that makes people want it the moment they read it.

---

**面試官:** 你對我們公司有什麼了解？  
*(Nǐ duì wǒ men gōng sī yǒu shén me liǎo jiě?)*  
→ What do you know about our company?

**Clark:** 我知道貴公司在台灣市場很有影響力，我對你們的品牌方向很感興趣，希望能貢獻我在行銷文案上的經驗。  
*(Wǒ zhī dào guì gōng sī zài Táiwān shì chǎng hěn yǒu yǐng xiǎng lì, wǒ duì nǐ men de pǐn pái fāng xiàng hěn gǎn xìng qù, xī wàng néng gòng xiàn wǒ zài xíng xiāo wén àn shàng de jīng yàn.)*  
→ I know your company has strong influence in the Taiwan market. I'm very interested in your brand direction and hope to contribute my experience in marketing copywriting.

---

### Pronunciation Focus

- **貴公司** *(guì gōng sī)* — "your esteemed company" — polite TW business phrase, use it in interviews
- **擅長** *(shàn cháng)* — "to be good at / specialize in" — stronger than 會 (huì), better for interviews
- **轉化** *(zhuǎn huà)* — "to transform/convert" — key verb for describing copywriting work

---

### Follow-up Questions (Practice Answering)

1. 你有沒有管理過團隊的經驗？  
   *(Nǐ yǒu méi yǒu guǎn lǐ guò tuán duì de jīng yàn?)*  
   → Have you had any experience managing a team?

2. 你期望的薪水是多少？  
   *(Nǐ qī wàng de xīn shuǐ shì duō shǎo?)*  
   → What is your expected salary?

3. 你為什麼離開上一份工作？  
   *(Nǐ wèi shén me lí kāi shàng yī fèn gōng zuò?)*  
   → Why are you leaving your previous job?

*(Try drafting your own answers for each — even 1–2 sentences each is great practice.)*
